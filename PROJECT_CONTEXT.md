# Project Context

## What This SaaS Is

WACRM is being converted from a self-hostable WhatsApp CRM template into a managed multi-client WhatsApp AI chatbot SaaS MVP for small businesses.

The product should become a simple WhatsApp AI sales assistant. It should answer customer questions, save conversations, detect buying intent, and help businesses follow up with potential buyers without becoming a complicated enterprise CRM.

## Target Customers

The first customers are small businesses in Botswana and Southern Africa, such as electronics shops, beauty businesses, repair services, delivery businesses, food sellers, local retailers, and service providers that already receive customer enquiries on WhatsApp.

These businesses need practical help:

- Reply faster on WhatsApp.
- Share prices, product/service information, location, opening hours, payment, and delivery details.
- Avoid missing serious buyers.
- Keep basic conversation history.
- Update their own products and FAQs without asking the SaaS operator every time.

## MVP Direction

Manual onboarding comes first. The SaaS operator creates each business, enters WhatsApp credentials/settings, chooses the package, and sets limits/features.

Business owners should then maintain normal business content themselves:

- Business description
- Location
- Opening hours
- Services
- FAQs
- Products/services
- Prices
- Stock/availability text
- Delivery instructions
- Payment/order instructions

## Packages

### Starter Bot

Price: P1500/month

Purpose: for small businesses that mainly want WhatsApp auto-replies.

Limits and features:

- Up to 1500 AI replies/month
- Up to 20 products/services
- AI WhatsApp auto-replies
- Business info and FAQs
- Product/FAQ editing from the business dashboard
- Basic conversation inbox
- Bot ON/OFF
- 1 business user/admin
- Manual onboarding by super admin
- Lead Lite only

Lead Lite means the system can detect buying intent, save lead-related metadata internally if practical, and show simple interest indicators inside the conversation such as "Customer asked about Game Projector." It does not include a full leads page, lead board, statuses, export, reminders, staff assignment, or advanced lead analytics.

### Growth Bot + Leads

Price: P3000/month

Purpose: for businesses that receive many enquiries and need to follow up with serious buyers.

Limits and features:

- Up to 5000 AI replies/month
- Up to 100 products/services
- Everything in Starter
- Full leads dashboard
- Lead statuses: New, Contacted, Won, Lost
- Lead notes
- Lead filters
- Lead source conversation/message
- Manual reply from dashboard
- Turn bot off for a specific conversation if practical
- Multiple users/staff accounts if existing account structure supports it
- Better analytics if practical
- Priority support label/setting if practical

### Custom

Purpose: manually configured clients.

Super admin can manually set:

- Monthly AI reply limit
- Product/service limit
- Feature flags
- Full Leads availability
- Other package-specific settings

## Important Package Rule

All packages allow the business to edit products, FAQs, services, prices, and business info from their dashboard.

The package difference is not whether the business can edit content. The difference is limits and lead-management power.

## Future Plans

Meta Embedded Signup is planned later for self-serve WhatsApp onboarding. Do not implement it in the MVP first pass.

Billing is planned later. Do not implement billing now. Package selection and limits are controlled manually by super admin for the MVP.

## Product North Star

Keep it practical and sellable:

Starter answers customers.

Growth answers customers and helps close leads.

Manual onboarding first. Embedded Signup later. Billing later.
